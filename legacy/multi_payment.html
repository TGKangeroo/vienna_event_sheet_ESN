How to enable multiple payment methods:

  1. Add a question called "Payment Method"
  2. Make this question either a dropdown or radiobutton
  3. Add the payment options to the question like for example "Office,PayPal"
  4. Fill in the financial information for your payment method

How to enable a different email per payment method:

  1. Enable the option "Send registration emails based on payment type" in the scripting options
  2. Make an email and give it a subject, in this example let's take "Registration"
  3. Make one copy per payment method you are offering and add "_payment method" to the subject. To continue with our previous example, the respective subjects would now be "Registration_Office" and "Registration_PayPal"
  4. Customise the emails to what you'd like the registered person to receive.
    • You can use [paypal_link] in your email to generate a payment link for PayPal.
    • You can use [bank_transfer] in your email to display the bank transfer details.
    • You can use [office] in your email to display the address of your office.
  5. Write the subject without the payment method in "Registration email name" in the scripting options, in our example you have to write "Registration" there.
