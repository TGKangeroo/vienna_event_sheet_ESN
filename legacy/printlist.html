If you want to choose what information you'd like to see on your printable list, follow the steps below:
  1. Read these steps
  2. Close this pop-up screen, NOT THE WHOLE SPREADSHEET
  3. Navigate to the print list tab, you can find this on the bottom of the spreadsheet
  4. Once you're on the print list tab, select the information you want from the dropdowns on the first row.
  5. If the information you want to print is not listed here, it means this information was not a question in your google form. Unfortunately this spreadsheet is not clairvoyant, it can only give you info you asked it to collect.
  6. You can then print the list by going to file>print in the top navigation.
